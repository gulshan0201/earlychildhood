Why Drama is Important?

Drama enhances children's communication skills, self-confidence, and emotional intelligence. It provides a creative way to explore different perspectives and express emotions in a safe environment.

Puppetry in Learning

Puppetry is an engaging tool for storytelling and role-playing, helping children develop language skills and creativity. It makes learning more interactive and enjoyable.

Drama Activities for Kids

Simple activities like role-playing, improvisation, and dramatized storytelling can significantly contribute to a child's cognitive and social development.
